Flour 2 cups
Salt 1 tsp.
Baking Powder 1 1/2 tbsp.
Sugar 1 tbsp.
Milk 1 2/3 cups
Egg 2
Melted Butter 1/3 cup
Vanilla Extract 1 tsp.
Directions
Combine the flour, salt, baking powder, and sugar in a large mixing bowl
Add the milk and the eggs. Whisk to combine
Pour the melted butter and vanilla into the batter and stir until mostly smooth
